Why just vacation when you can EcoScape?

An EcoScape is more than just a vacation

If you’d like to reduce your footprint a bit, get closer to nature and enjoy the many pleasures of eco retreats, then consider an EcoScape, where sustainability and ecological responsibility are an important part of each home, and where there are countless opportunities to experience incredible natural beauty and unique bio-diversity at each location.

Our Homes

We believe in finding the intersection of sustainability and adventure. We aim to provide the closest experience to nature without harming the environment in any way. All while taking the time to rest and unplug from the daily hustle and to focus on ourselves, our loved ones, and the beauty of nature.

Disconnect From Society and the Grid

Enjoy your getaway while saving the environment. Each Home is 100% off grid running on renewable resources.

Comforts of Home, While Out In Nature

Each home has a comfortable queen bed with fresh linens and pillows. Our beds are always in view of a window, so you can fall asleep under the stars and wake up with a view.

Explore the Unkown

Each Home is in close proximity of the best parks, nature preserves, and landmarks the area has to offer.

Solitude of Nature

Lock away your phone, connect with loved ones, and enjoy quiet time in nature. You’ll have everything you need to truly relax.
